Our Naval Legacy

War 124: The Beginning

What would become KolonialMarine began as a simple idea between two friends during War 124. Myself and Kuroko (known by many names throughout the wars) saw the greatness in a strong navy

We preferred the sleek designs and superior aesthetics of Colonial ships which is why we decided to deflect from the Wardens

The Kuroko Principle

Kuroko earned his position through that rare combination of:

  • Actual competence when he could be bothered to wake up
  • A mysterious ability to be exactly where needed (usually after three reminder pings)
  • The tactical patience of someone who'd rather let others do the work

To this day, Kuroko remains our silent Admiral, awake for critical operations, and never, ever using voice comms.

War 125: Official Founding

The KolonialMarine was formally established at the start of War 125. What began as two friends discussing naval stuff had grown into a full-fledged regiment with:

  • Regular naval operations
  • A core group of dedicated naval specialists
